Mystery: The Troubled Tincture

2 hours | 8 Person | Storycrafting

Trouble brews alongside the famously well-crafted waters at Bramble Twist Botanicals. A cutting betrayal shreds through the close-knit team of experts that comprise the distillation staff. Someone has poisoned a prized batch of rose water, set to be shipped to an elite client. With luck, the Quality Assurance Chemist caught the batch before it left the distillery. As trusted members of the distillery crew, it falls to you and your colleagues to investigate this act of in-house treachery. Equipped with knowledge honed from years working alongside one another, you set out to uncover who among your trusted comrades could be capable of such a sinister act and why.

Your search leads you through the many nooks and crannies of the distillery, gathering clues wherever the saboteur may have left traces of their work. In the arrangement of the bottle labels, the locked cabinets housing the rarest flavors, the logbooks tracking each stage of distillation - somewhere amidst the familiar details lies a crucial revelation.

With suspicion cast on the team you once trusted like family, you must look beyond the bonds of camaraderie to uncover the truth. From deciphering cryptic notes left amongst the equipment to unlocking puzzles that safeguard the distillery's secrets, stay true to your knowledge of one another and it will illuminate the way.

The future reputation of Bramble Twist Botanicals hangs in the balance. With care and cunning, can you root out the poisoner in your midst, and restore harmony to the hallowed halls of botanical brewing? The chase is on, and the culprit is closer than you think.
